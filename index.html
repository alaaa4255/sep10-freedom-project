BUSINESS

INTRODUCTION

In the world, there are many types of businesses. These businesses use various software and hardware to operate efficiently. Software includes apps and websites they rely on, while hardware refers to the physical tools and equipment used in daily operations.

HARDWARE

SERVERS

  • Host websites, emails, and databases.
  • Provide security and reliability.
STORAGE DEVICES
  • Backup data and files securely.
  • Allow access to information anytime.
  • Ensure business continuity.
CARD READERS
  • Process credit and debit transactions.
  • Secure and document sales.
  • Speed up payment processes.
PERIPHERALS
  • Scanners and printers for documentation.
  • Enhance office functionality.
  • Improve business efficiency.
