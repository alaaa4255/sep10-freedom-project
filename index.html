BUSINESS

INTRODUCTION

In the world there is so many types of business. These business tend to use a variety of different things to run it. These things are both software and hardware. More specifically, Software are the apps and websites these business use. The hardware is the pieces that object they are using is made of.

HARDWARE

SERVERS
  • Servers are powerful computers designed to handle and process large amounts of data, manage network resources, and provide services to other computers or devices on a network.
  • Servers are the backbone of hosting websites, email systems, databases, etc.
  • Servers can really help a business with the way it adds security and a reliable source to lean back on.
STORAGE DEVICES
  • Storage devices just help create backups for your data and files.
  • They help you have the ability to access your information anytime.
  • Backs up any type of business data.
  • Secure all business dealings.
CARD READERS
  • Card readers is a device that accepts credit and debit cards transactions.
  • Card readers provide high security while documenting customer transactions in case anything happens.
  • Card readers can make the process go way faster since it works smoothly.
PERIPHERALS
  • Peripherals help business scan documents.
  • Print out papers.
  • Make presentation.
  • They increase the function of computers.
